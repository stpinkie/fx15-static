Meet the Team

We believe in magic.

 


 

DANIEL “ST. PINKIE” ANTHONY

THE ONE WHO DOES WHAT HAS TO BE DONE

A jack of all trades, Daniel “St. Pinkie” Anthony sent ponies back to school at Canterlot University and rocked Singapore at The Cloudsdale Splash. As deputy chairperson of both these previous conventions, he is set to bring the magic to the shores of Malaysia with The Friendship Express. A brony since September 2011, Daniel quickly fell in love with the show and fandom. As an active member of the community he wants to make the best of what he has and give back to the community at large from all that he has learned from the journey.

– “It’s time to stop tolerating and start accepting!”

 

 

CHARLIE “MUFFYDERP”

THE ONE WHO KEEPS EVERYTHING RUNNING IN ORDER

Charlie has been a fan of MLP:FiM since July 2011 and thinks that the Internet is not a nice place for little fillies. He found the pony cartoon appealing because it provides much needed positivity in a harsh, cruel cyberspace in addition to it being happy and nostalgic of cartoons from the 1980s’. He wishes to expand more on the fandom’s positive side, by bringing together like-minded fans for gatherings and fun activities. He hopes to be more involved and do charity work based on My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic in the future.

– “Which part of ‘Friendship is Magic’ don’t you understand?”

 

 

MEELZ

THE ONE WHO MAKES THE ENTERTAINMENT ENTERTAINING

PonyCon AU, Canterlot Univerisity and The Cloudsdale Splash would not have been the same without Meelz. After gracing the stage, he is working to put together a rocking performance at The Friendship Express that will blow everypony’s mind. Coming from a background of Rock n’ Roll, Rap and a bit of everything else, one cannot question that he knows his music. You can expect no dull moments when you’re living it up at The Friendship Express.

– “You gotta see beyond the lighted stage!”
