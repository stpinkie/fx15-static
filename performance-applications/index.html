Harmony 6 Guidelines

 

 

What’s this about?

 

The Friendship Express is a convention for the community
by the community. Thus, we would like to provide those who are
actively contributing awesome things to the community with an
avenue to take their work to the next step. Its our way of saying
thank you for making the universe of fan content one of the reasons
we stay in the fandom.

 

First come, first served.
Performance applications close 6 February 2015.
Subject to approval by the committee.

 

TFE WILL NOT IMPOSE PERFORMANCE FEES. HOWEVER, PERFORMERS NEED TO ENSURE THAT ALL PERFORMANCE
ROYALTIES, EQUIPMENT RENTALS AND PERFORMANCE PERMITS ARE COVERED AT TIME OF PERFORMANCE.

 

What you can do

 

Here are a few things you can do at The Friendship Express,
but don’t limit yourself to this! We would love to see
what you can come up with!

 

– Do a live performance
– Sing covers of MLP music
– Host a giveaway of your artwork or craft
– Host a panel
– Announce a project you are working on
– Present a project you are working on to gain support and help
– Play games with the crowd
– Show off your artwork

 

However, the following activities will
not be entertained.

– Making a speech
– Doing a shoutout session
– Telemarketing
– Presenting inappropriate content
– Anything that takes an excessive amount of time
– Non-pony related presentations/performances
(at least make it partially pony)
